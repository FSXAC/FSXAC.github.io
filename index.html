Hello!

I graduated from University of British Columbia in 2022 (M.A.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering). My research focus was on CPU hardware security and I worked on side-channel attacks and defenses for modern processors.

I previously worked at Solidigm, performing emulation validation on SSD controllers. I had co-op terms at Intel NSG (SSD division that was spun off to form Solidigm) as FPGA Engineering intern, EA - BioWare as UI/UX Programmer Intern, and VitalMechanics as a Software Developer Intern.

In my free time, I enjoy analog photography, video games, and tinkering with new technologies.
